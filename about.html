A note from Mrs Cave

At Oakridge, we believe every student deserves to feel seen, supported, and excited to learn. We work hard to build a school culture where curiosity is encouraged, effort is celebrated, and every child has the confidence to grow — academically, socially, and personally.


Our doors are always open, and we're honoured to walk this journey alongside our students and their families.
